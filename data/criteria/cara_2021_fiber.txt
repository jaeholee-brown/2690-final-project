TABLE S1. Eligibility criteria for studies considered for review

IMPORTANT STAGE RULE
  At L1 (title/abstract review), do not exclude solely because a record appears to be a
  narrative review, commentary, conference proceeding, non-English publication, mixed
  ICU/non-ICU population, or an RCT with a fiber-based placebo/comparator if the title
  and abstract otherwise appear relevant. Forward such records to L2 for final
  determination whenever the population and fiber-related enteral exposure appear
  potentially on target.

CATEGORY
  Study design
    Inclusion criteria:
      Any clinical study or case study:
      - Randomized controlled trials
      - Non-randomized controlled trials including quasi-experimental and
        controlled before-and-after studies
      - Prospective cohort studies
      - Retrospective cohort studies
      - Case-controlled studies
      - Nested case-controlled studies
      - Case reports/case studies
      - Mendelian randomization studies
      - Uncontrolled trials
      - Cross-sectional studies
    Exclusion criteria:
      - Narrative reviews
      - Letters to the editor (as long as they do not include a case study)
      - Protocols
      - Background reading
      - Meta-analyses
      - Systematic reviews

  Study duration
    Inclusion criteria:
      - No restriction
    Exclusion criteria:
      - NA

  Sample size
    Inclusion criteria:
      - No restriction
    Exclusion criteria:
      - NA

  Intervention/exposure
    Inclusion criteria:
      - Any dietary fiber including prebiotics, probiotics, synbiotics, and
        multi-fiber formulas
      - Enteral feeding (oral, sublingual, rectal)
    Exclusion criteria:
      - Interventions other than dietary fiber
      - Parenteral nutrition

  Comparator
    Inclusion criteria:
      - Comparisons across different dietary fibers
      - Comparisons of dietary fibers to other foods/beverages
      - Comparison(s) to a non-fiber placebo
      - No comparator
    Exclusion criteria:
      - For RCTs, exclude if the placebo is fiber-based (i.e., inulin)

  Outcomes
    Inclusion criteria:
      - Any health-related outcomes defined by the original research
    Exclusion criteria:
      - Other outcomes

  Date of publication
    Inclusion criteria:
      - No restriction
    Exclusion criteria:
      - NA

  Publication status
    Inclusion criteria:
      - Articles published in peer-reviewed journals
    Exclusion criteria:
      - Articles not published in peer-reviewed journals, including unpublished
        data, manuscript reports, abstracts, pre-prints, and conference
        proceedings

  Language of publication
    Inclusion criteria:
      - English
    Exclusion criteria:
      - Languages other than English

  Country
    Inclusion criteria:
      - No restriction
    Exclusion criteria:
      - NA

  Study participants
    Inclusion criteria:
      - Any human subjects
    Exclusion criteria:
      - Non-human subjects
      - In vitro

  Age of study participants
    Inclusion criteria:
      - No age restriction at this time
    Exclusion criteria:
      - N/A

  Health status of participants
    Inclusion criteria:
      - 100% ICU patients or critically ill patients
      - No disease restriction at this time
    Exclusion criteria:
      - Non-ICU patients or noncritically ill patients
